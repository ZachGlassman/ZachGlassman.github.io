What is Beamer?

Beamer is the document class used for presentations in LaTeX. It is the rough equivalent of Microsoft Powerpoint except it produces pdf documents and offers all the other advantages of LaTeX. You can think of it as an easy way to generate many pages of LaTeX as frames (the equivalent of slides).
