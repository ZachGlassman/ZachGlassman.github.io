Some of my Math

I decided sometime in my Sophomore year of college that I would do my homework in LaTeX. Sometimes in the middle of my Junior year I decided I would liveTex my notes. Here are notes from Analysis I (partial) taken from Rudin, Complex Analysis, Analysis II and Measure Theory. I do not guarentee correctness and I am sure there are typos. However, I believe they are mostly small typographical errors and not mathematical ones.

Real Analysis I

These notes cover continuity, including uniform continuity of functions, sequences nad series of functions, differentiability and integrability in one dimension. They are following Rudin.

Real Analysis II

These notes cover the space of continuous functions and extend differentiability and integrability to arbitrary dimension. Also, some fixed point theory, approximation theory and contraction theory.

Complex Analsyis

These notes cover the basis of complex analysis including sequences and series of complex numbers, Cauchy's Theorem, Laruent expanions, and the calculus of Residues.

Graduate Real Analysis I (Measure Theory)

These notes cover measure, mostly in Euclidean space and Lebesgue integration.
