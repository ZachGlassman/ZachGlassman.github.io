About Me

Graduate School

I am a second year graduate student at the University of Maryland, College Park studying Chemical physics. I work at the Joint Quantum Institute which is a collaboration between UMD and the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), a government research organization located in Gaithersburg, MD. I am a member of the Laser Cooling and Trapping group and work under Dr. Paul Lett.

My course work to date has focused on Quantum Mechanics, Quantum Chemistry, and Statistical Mechanics/Thermodynamics. I am working towards a Ph.D which I hope to complete in a few years. I am interested in problems that arise at the union of chemistry and physics, namely the quantum description of atoms and molecules.

Undergradate

I recieved my undergraduate degree from Pomona College in Claremont, CA where I doubled majored in Physics and Mathematics. I both wrote a thesis and published a paper in a peer reviewed journal in each subject.
