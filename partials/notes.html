Here are some notes I have compiled during my education. I do not guarentee completeness, but they should be mostly correct.

S=Real Analysis I

First course in undergraduate analysis. These notes follow Rudin and contain:

  • Continuity and uniform continuity of sequences and functions
  • Differential in one dimension
  • Integration in one dimension
Notes

Real Analysis II

Second course in undergraduate analysis.

  • Space of Continuous Functions
  • Differentiation in n-dimensions
  • Integration in n-dimensions
  • Fixed Point Theory
  • Approximation Theory
Notes

Complex Analysis

First course in undergraduate Complex Analysis

  • Complex numbers
  • Series and sequences of complex numbers
  • Cauchy's theorem and applications
  • Laurent and Taylor expansions
  • Calculus of Residues
